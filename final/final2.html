My Birth and Childhood

I, admittedly, didn’t have the best childhood. I was born artificially in a lab made by humans, alongside 3 others, and as such I didn’t really have a mother or father. As a result of my artificial birth, I gained human intelligence and lifespan. The humans had always treated me well, and they even offered me many resources to help me learn more about the world. Through it, I developed a curiosity that couldn’t be sated by mere books. When I turned 18, I bid farewell to my human caretakers, and went out into the world to explore its secrets for myself.
