RETICULATED PYTHON

wikipedia for python
  • Reticulated pythons are the longest snakes on the planet, and the third heaviest.
  • It is listed as "least concern" by the ICUN because of its large distribution.
  • Reticulated pythons are one of the few snakes with the capacity to kill and prey on humans.
  • They are excellent swimmers, they have been reported far out sea.
  • They are sometimes sold as exotic pets.
